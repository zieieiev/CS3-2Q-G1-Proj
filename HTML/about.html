Our Mission

At CAT, we are committed to preventing animal abuse and promoting the welfare of all animals. Our mission is to educate the public, advocate for stronger animal protection laws, and provide resources for those in need.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and advocacy are at the core of our values. We believe in treating all living beings with respect and dignity and work tirelessly to ensure their protection.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate animal lovers and dedicated professionals who work collaboratively to make a difference in the lives of animals. Together, we strive to create a world where all animals are safe and loved.
