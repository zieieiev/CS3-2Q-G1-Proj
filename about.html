About CAT

CAT (Call for Animal Treatment) is a non-profit organization dedicated to fighting against animal cruelty and promoting the humane treatment of animals. Our mission is to raise awareness about animal welfare issues and to provide support to those in need.

Our Vision

We envision a world where all animals are treated with compassion and respect. We aim to educate the public about the importance of animal welfare and to advocate for stronger laws protecting animals from abuse and neglect.

Our Programs

  • Educational Workshops
  • Community Outreach
  • Adoption Drives
  • Fundraising Events

Join Us

We invite you to join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of animals. Whether through volunteering, donating, or spreading awareness, every effort counts!
